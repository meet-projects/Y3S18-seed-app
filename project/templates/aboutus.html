About ArtFlict



Artflict is a raising startup company created by Y3 in MEET (Middle East Entrepreneurs Of Tomorrow). Our main

aim is to create a more compassionate and tolerate society where are is used more frequently for expression

and therapy. We are trying to help people suffering from the Palestinian- Israeli conflict in their daily lives

to express themselves in a friendly artistic way. We are an app in which people express their emotion and artist

empathize with their stories by creating related art
